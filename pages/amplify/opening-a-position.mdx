# Opening a Position

Select the market you would like to trade by clicking `Trade` followed by `Amplify` and choose your desired market.

<img src="/assets/amplify-opening-a-position-1.png" alt="" width="600" height="auto"></img>

Select your desired leverage ratio and input the amount of collateral you want to deposit.

<img src="/assets/amplify-opening-a-position-2.png" alt="" width="600" height="auto"></img>

Review your position details and confirm the transaction.

<img src="/assets/amplify-opening-a-position-3.png" alt="" width="600" height="auto"></img>

Once confirmed, your position will be opened and you can monitor it in the Positions tab.

<img src="/assets/amplify-opening-a-position-4.png" alt="" width="600" height="auto"></img>
