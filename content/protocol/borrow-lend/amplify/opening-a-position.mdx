# Opening a Position

<Steps>
### Navigate to Earn
From the top navigation panel, go to `Earn` followed by `Amplify` and choose your desired market.

<img
  src="/assets/amplify-opening-a-position-1.png"
  alt=""
  width="600"
  height="auto"
></img>

### Select leverage ratio and amount
Select your desired leverage ratio and input the amount of collateral you want to deposit.

<img
  src="/assets/amplify-opening-a-position-2.png"
  alt=""
  width="600"
  height="auto"
></img>

### Review
Review your position details and confirm the transaction.

<img
  src="/assets/amplify-opening-a-position-3.png"
  alt=""
  width="600"
  height="auto"
></img>

### Confirm
Once confirmed, your position will be opened and you can monitor it in the Positions tab.

<img
  src="/assets/amplify-opening-a-position-4.png"
  alt=""
  width="600"
  height="auto"
></img>
</Steps>
